Challenge Yourself to Go Further at HCIS

Harbor City International School (HCIS) challenges students to go further through rigorous academics that have earned our us recognition as the #20 high school in Minnesota and #1 in Duluth. Our unique symposium experiences exploring diverse subjects, comprehensive arts programs, and meaningful service learning partnerships with local nonprofits combine for an incredible experience for every student. At HCIS, our students discover their potential and achieve more than they ever thought possible.

4 Ways Character Matters at Harbor City International School

At Harbor City International School, character education is built into daily life through our 5R’s: Respect, Responsibility, Relationship, Reach, and Resilience. These values guide how students treat others, tackle challenges, and contribute to their community - both in and out of school. By centering character alongside academics, we’re helping students grow into thoughtful, capable, and compassionate individuals.
